purple is "running process on the command"

Manuel Eberl (Jan 20 2021 at 11:00):

If it is just purple indefinitely, it means that it has not terminated yet. As I explained in the other thread, that means that the proof is not yeat complicated. The ‘No subgoals!’ you see is provisional in the sense that it assumes that the proof is valid (which it isn't if the simp never terminates).
