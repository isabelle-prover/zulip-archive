Dear potential CPP authors,

Please note that, if you intend to submit a paper to CPP 2021, you
would need to register it with an abstract very soon, namely by

16 September 2020 at 23:59 AoE (UTC-12h)

The submission can be uploaded by 22 September 2020 at 23:59 AoE (UTC-12h).

Finally, one piece of news we can share is that now it’s official: CPP
2021 will be a virtual meeting, where all papers are presented online!
The updated call for papers can be found at

https://popl21.sigplan.org/home/CPP-2021#Call-for-Papers

Best wishes,
Andrei & Catalin
(CPP 2021 program chairs)
