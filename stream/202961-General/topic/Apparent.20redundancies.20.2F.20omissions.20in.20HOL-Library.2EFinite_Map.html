Topic: Apparent redundancies / omissions in HOL-Library.Finite_Map

Alex Mobius (Feb 24 2026 at 17:13):

I've been using HOL-Library.Finite_Map in my theories and have noticed a couple of warts:

  • Some definitions seem redundant, perhaps added before their counterparts in Map were added - map_restrict_set is Map.restrict_map with arguments swapped, set_of_map is Map.graph. The corresponding fmap_restrict also doesn't have any syntax to it. The common definitions have more lemmas that could be transferred, but I don't know how much value there is in making the aliases explicit.
  • fmap_subseteq is missing partial order lemmas (unsure if order instantiation is warranted here - it exists for unbounded maps). These partial lemmas are trivial to prove by transfer', but surprisingly tricky using just the provided lemmas (even reflexivity is non-trivial!) - they should IMO definitely be included.
    I'm happy to contribute on these counts, but don't really know what the model for that is - I hope I don't need a scholarship at TUM :).
