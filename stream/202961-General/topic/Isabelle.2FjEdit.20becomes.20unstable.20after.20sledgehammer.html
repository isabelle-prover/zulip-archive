Yosuke Ito (Mar 08 2026 at 23:51):

I would like to correct my previous comment.
Even with a new MacBook with 32 GB of RAM, the same behavior reproduced. I asked AI for a solution, and it suggested setting ML_OPTIONS="--maxheap 8g". Now the problem appears to be truly settled.
As for veriT, as @Fabian Huch suggested, it sometimes runs idle in the background of Isabelle/jEdit, which causes a delay in continuous checking. In that case, I kill the veriT process from the Activity Monitor on my MacBook.
