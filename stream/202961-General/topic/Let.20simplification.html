Mathias Fleury (Oct 12 2023 at 05:07):

Think of

A = (
let x = (if a then f y else g y) in
let y = x * x + x in
2*y * y)

becoming

 (a ⟶ A = (2 * (f y * f y) + 2 * f y) * (f y * f y + f y)) ∧
    (¬ a ⟶ A = (2 * (g y * g y) + 2 * g y) * (g y * g y + g y))

if x and y are meaningfull names, they are much better
