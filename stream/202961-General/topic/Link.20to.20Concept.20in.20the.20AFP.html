Fabian Huch (Sep 24 2024 at 07:21):

You can link to AFP content in two ways:

  • link to the AFP webpage by clicking on the element in the sidebar, and copy the browser url, e.g. nrest
  • look up the underlying html presentation, and find the element id there, e.g. nrest

None of these are stable identifiers though and might not work in the next AFP release. I do want to have stable identifiers but we're not there yet.
