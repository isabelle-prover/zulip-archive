Topic: Narrowing the search space

Gergely Buday (Aug 28 2025 at 14:17):

You can use Sledgehammer effectively if the search space is small enough.

We can narrow the search space by

  • instantiating theorems using [of ...] and [OF ...]
  • unfolding theorems by [unfolded ...]
  • writing calculational proofs so that the individual equations give rise to a smaller search space than the original equation
  • using in-proof defines, simplifying the expressions in the proof
  • giving a good using section with relevant lemmas

What else do you have in mind for this?
