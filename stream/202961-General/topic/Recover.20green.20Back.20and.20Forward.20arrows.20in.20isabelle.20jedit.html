Topic: Recover green Back and Forward arrows in isabelle jedit

Alexandra Graß (Jan 21 2026 at 14:47):

The new distribution Isabelle2025-2 comes without the two green arrows allowing one to navigate back to previous locations, for example after clicking on and following the link to a definition or theorem placed elsewhere. I use this option quiet often and was sad not to find it anymore. Here's my fix:

  • Go to Plugins > Plugin Manager…
  • Click on Install tab and search for Navigator. Activate the check mark next to it (will automatically select Code2HTML as well). Click Install.
  • Close Plugin Manager
  • Go to Utilities > Global Options…
  • Navigate to Tool Bar. Remove (and add) buttons as needed.
  • Click +. In the following dialogue, select the Navigator plugin, then search for the desired command/macro and match it with the appropriate icon. Add by clicking OK.
  • Repeat with the anti-command. Add sepators as needed. The result may look like this.

Wanted to share this in case other people encounter the same issue :)
