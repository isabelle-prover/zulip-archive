  • competition: https://competition.isabelle.systems
  • dev: isabelle-dev.sketis.net
  • dev-email: https://mailmanbroy.informatik.tu-muenchen.de/pipermail/isabelle-dev/
  • doc: https://isabelle.in.tum.de/documentation.html
  • email: https://lists.cam.ac.uk/pipermail/cl-isabelle-users/index.html
  • isar: https://isabelle.in.tum.de/dist/Isabelle2019/doc/isar-ref.pdf
  • ml-tips: https://nms.kcl.ac.uk/christian.urban/Cookbook/
  • repo: https://isabelle-dev.sketis.net/source/isabelle/

Please let us know any further suggestions.
