Lukas Stevens (Aug 22 2024 at 07:31):

Unfortunately, the profiling situation in Isabelle is very unsatisfactory. There is very rudimentary profiling in Isabelle/ML that gives you a list of functions where the time is spent. Those results are not very accurate, though, and often even misleading. So I recommend not spending any time on that. The only other way is to time functions the old school way.
