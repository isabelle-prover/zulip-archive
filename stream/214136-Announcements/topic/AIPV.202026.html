Hello everyone,

Please, consider joining AIPV 2026, a workshop on AI, theorem proving,
and formal verification, co-located with FM 2026 in Tokyo.
Topics include AI-guided proof search, higher-order logics, benchmarks,
and practical experiences with interactive theorem provers such as Isabelle/
HOL.
Key information:
• Co-located with FM 2026 (Tokyo, May 18 or 19, 2026)
• Focus: soundness-aware AI for proving and verification
• Welcomes published work, extended abstracts, tools, benchmarks
• Submission deadline: March 15, 2026
