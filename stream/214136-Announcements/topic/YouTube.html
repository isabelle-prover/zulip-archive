Dear Isabelle users,

Towards the end of 2024, I began using AI avatars to create podcast-like videos about research papers on theorem proving, as well as topics related to AI/ML and programming languages.

Enjoy watching them here:

  • ProofCast in English
  • ProofCast in Spanish

I also aim to present these podcasts in multiple languages to help overcome language barriers. While I take basic measures to accurately represent the work and avoid harmful or incorrect connotations, Japanese is my only native language. If you notice any problematic expressions, I would greatly appreciate your constructive feedback.

At the moment, I’m introducing papers that I co-authored as the lead author. If you have a peer-reviewed academic paper on theorem proving, and (AI, programming languages, computer architecture, or CPU design) that you would like included in the series, feel free to let me know.
