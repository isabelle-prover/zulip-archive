Manuel Eberl (Jul 16 2021 at 19:22):

I don't know of a proper way to do this, but what I often do is to define a dummy function like

definition f :: "'a :: wellorder ⇒ 'a" where
  "f x = x"

and then do

term "f (x :: nat)"

and see if that gives me an error or not.
