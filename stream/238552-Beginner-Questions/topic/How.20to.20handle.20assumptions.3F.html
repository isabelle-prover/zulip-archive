Gergely Buday (Jul 26 2023 at 15:03):

ChatGPT came up with a simpler one. After some cosmetic changes, it became

lemma double_negation_intro: "P ⟶ ¬¬P"
proof (rule impI)
  assume "P"
  show "¬¬P"
  proof (rule notI)
    assume "¬P"
    with ‹P› show False by contradiction
  qed
qed
