Simon Roßkopf (Feb 27 2022 at 16:29):

The precondition in the rule you want to apply does unify with the one in your goal. To compare the two:

{λs. 0 < s ''a'' ∧ 0 < s ''b'' ∧ gcd n m = gcd (s ''a'') (s ''b'')}
{λs. (s ''a'' = n) ∧ (s ''b'' = m) ∧ (n > 0) ∧ (m > 0)}
