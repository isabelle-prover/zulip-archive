Gergely Buday (Aug 14 2023 at 09:46):

I have collected some sources for Isabelle knowledge:

  • The docs in the distribution: Isabelle/Isar Reference Manual etc, including the old Tutorial
  • Concrete Semantics Part I
  • The code base of the distribution and of the Archive of Formal Proofs
  • The Isabelle Community Cookbook
  • The Isabelle Community Wiki
  • The Isabelle Programming Tutorial by Christian Urban et al
  • The Proofcraft Blog
  • Blumson's Isabelle for Philosopers
  • Breitner's Theorem Beweiser Praktikum
  • Interactive Theorem Proving using Isabelle/HOL at Innsbruck
  • Isabelle/HOL Exercises at TU Munich
  • The archives of the Isabelle mailing list and of Isabelle Zulipchat
  • The Serapis search engine
  • Correctness Proofs of Distributed Systems with Isabelle, a talk by Martin Klepmann

I would love to hear additions to this list.
