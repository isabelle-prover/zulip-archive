Mathias Fleury (Nov 05 2024 at 12:24):

OF conjI

Mathias Fleury (Nov 05 2024 at 12:24):

Full example:

context
  fixes A B :: bool
begin
definition t where
  ‹t ≡ (A ∧ B)›

lemma H: ‹t ⟹ A›
  sorry
thm H[unfolded t_def, OF conjI]
