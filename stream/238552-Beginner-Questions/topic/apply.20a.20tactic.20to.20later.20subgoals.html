Topic: apply a tactic to later subgoals

Rivdo (Feb 26 2024 at 03:37):

During the proof, I have nnn subgoals, is there any format that can apply the current tactic to the specified subgoals nnn, or not prove the subgoals in the order they are parsed by the prover? Thanks in advance.

Yong Kiam (Feb 26 2024 at 03:40):

defer n and prefer n shuffle the list of pending goals: defer puts off sub-
goal n to the end of the list (n = 1 by default), while prefer brings
sub-goal n to the front.
