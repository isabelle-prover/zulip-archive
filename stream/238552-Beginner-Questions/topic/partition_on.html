Mathias Fleury (Oct 02 2022 at 15:40):

It is more a notation problem:

lemma ‹A≠B ⟹ disjoint {A,B} ⟷ A∩B = {}›
  by (auto simp: disjoint_def)

People usually implicitly mean A≠B when they writing {A,B}.
