TFP 2026 -- Call For Participation
(trendsfp.github.io [1])

Venue
TFPiE and TFP will take place in-person in Odense, Denmark, at the
University of
Southern Denmark.

Dates
Early registration: 31 December, 2025
Late registration: 20 January , 2026
TFPiE Workshop: Monday 26 January, 2026
TFP Symposium: Tuesday 27 - Thursday 29 January, 2026

The Symposium on Trends in Functional Programming (TFP) is an
international
forum for researchers with interests in all aspects of functional
programming,
taking a broad view of current and future trends in the area. It aspires
to be a
lively environment for presenting the latest research results, and other
contributions.

Keynote speakers
We are happy to have the following keynotes in the programme:

Alex Gerdes (TFPiE), Gothenburg University and Chalmers:
"Generating Feedback from Rewrite Strategies for Interactive
Exercises"
Joachim Breitner, Lean Focused Research Organization:
"Lean Highlights"
Ohad Kammar, University of Edinburgh:
"Synthetic Convolution"
András Kovács, Gothenburg University and Chalmers:
TBA

Programme

We have 13 paper presentations, as well as invited talks. A preliminary
programme schedule can be found here:
trendsfp.github.io/schedule.html [2]

Excursion and banquet
In the afternoon and evening of Wednesday January 28th we have an
excursion to
H.C. Andersen Museum, followed by a break before heading to Madklubben
for the
conference banquet.

Travel Scholarship

Thanks to the generous support from the Carlsberg Foundation, we are
able to
offer travel scholarships (DKK 3000 ~ USD 470 ~ EUR 400) to young
researchers
traveling to attend TFP 2026 in Odense, Denmark. Information on how to
apply
can be found here:
https://trendsfp.github.io/scholarship.html
