5th International Workshop on Formal Methods for Blockchains (FMBC) 2024 - Call for Participation

https://fmbc.gitlab.io/2024

April 07, 2024, Luxembourg City, Luxembourg, 09:00 - 18:00 CEST

Co-located with the European joint conferences on theory and practice of software (ETAPS 2024)

https://www.etaps.org/2024/

The FMBC workshop is a forum to identify theoretical and practical
approaches of formal methods for Blockchain technology. Topics
include, but are not limited to:

  • Formal models of Blockchain applications or concepts
  • Formal methods for consensus protocols
  • Formal methods for Blockchain-specific cryptographic primitives or protocols
  • Design and implementation of Smart Contract languages
  • Verification of Smart Contracts

This year, we will have 9 research talks and 2 additional lightning talks.
The program is available online:

https://easychair.org/smart-program/FMBC2024/

In addition, we are excited to have a keynote by Franck Cassez (https://franck44.github.io/) from Mantle (https://www.mantle.xyz/) about

Deductive verification of smart contracts

Registration

Registration to FMBC 2024 is done through ETAPS 2024:
https://www.etaps.org/2024/attending/
