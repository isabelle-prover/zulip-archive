The AI for Math Fund, sponsored by Renaissance Philanthropy and XTX
Markets, is a grant opportunity committing $9.2 million to research,
field-building and development of open-source tools and datasets in the
intersection of AI and mathematics. Projects related to AI for proof
assistants (including Isabelle) are encouraged to apply.
