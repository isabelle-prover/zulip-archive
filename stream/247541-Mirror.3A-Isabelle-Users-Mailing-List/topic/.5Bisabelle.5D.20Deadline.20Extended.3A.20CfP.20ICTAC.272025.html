Call for Papers - ICTAC 2025
https://ictac2025.digital-hub.sh/ <https://ictac2025.digital-hub.sh/>
International Colloquium on Theoretical Aspects of Computing
Marrakech, Morocco, November 24th to 28th, 2025

About ICTAC:

The ICTAC conference series aims at bringing together researchers and
practitioners from academia, industry, and government to present research and
exchange ideas and experiences within theoretical aspects of computing through
(formal) methods and tools for system development. ICTAC also aims to promote
research cooperation between developing and industrial countries.

Topics of Interest:

The conference concerns all aspects of theoretical computer science, including,
but not limited to:

* Formal languages and automata
* Semantics of programming languages
* Logic in computer science
* Lambda calculus, type theory, and category theory
* Domain-specific languages
* Theories of concurrency and mobility
* Theories of distributed computing
* Models of objects and components
* Coordination models
* Security and privacy
* Static analysis
* Software verification
* Timed, hybrid, and cyber-physical systems
* Verification, analysis and control synthesis
* Software testing
* Program generation and transformation
* Model checking and theorem-proving
* Quantum computing
* Trustworthy AI
* Machine learning algorithms
* Applications and experience

* All accepted papers will be published in Springer's Lecture Notes in
Computer Science.

* Springer sponsors the ICTAC 2025 Best Paper Award with a cash prize of 1.000
Euro.

* There will be a special issue for selected papers from ICTAC 2025 in the
Elsevier’s journal Theoretical Computer Science.
