Dear Logic Community,

to celebrate the winners of the first edition of the

  • Dov Gabbay Prize for Logic and Foundations (iloaf.org) *

Dale Miller and  Mirek Truszczynski

we will organize an Online Award Ceremony on

Wednesday, October 25, 15:00 (CET).

People interested to attend are invited to send a registration email to:
dgp@iloaf.org.
The Zoom link will be sent by email, but will also be published on Oct
25 on iloaf.org.

Program (~ 150 min.):

  1. Intro + Laudatio

  2. Talk: Dale Miller (Inria-Saclay and LIX, Ecole Polytechnique)
            "A system of inference based on proof search"

  3. Talk: Mirek Truszczynski (University of Kentucky)
            "The Road to Answer Set Programming"

  4. Talk: Dov Gabbay (King's College London, University of Luxembourg)
            "Negation as failure and failure in general"

Looking forward to see you there,

Emil Weydert and Alexander Steen (ILOAF organizers)
