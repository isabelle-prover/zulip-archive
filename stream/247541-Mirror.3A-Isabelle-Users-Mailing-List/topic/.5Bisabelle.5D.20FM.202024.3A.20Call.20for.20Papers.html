CALL FOR PAPERS

FM 2024 is the 26th international symposium on Formal Methods in a
series organized by Formal Methods Europe (FME).

FM 2024 features regular papers, tutorial papers, an industry day, an
embedded systems track and more.

The conference proceedings will be published OPEN ACCESS by Springer in
the LNCS series, as part of the FM subline.
