Dear Isabelle users,

the release process for Isabelle2025-1 (December 2025) is getting close to its
end.

Before everything becomes final and unchangeable, we have another release
candidate: https://isabelle.in.tum.de/website-Isabelle2025-1-RC4 --- there
have been notable changes that are listed on the blog entry
https://sketis.net/2025/release-candidates-for-isabelle2025-1

There is already an official release version of the Archive of Formal Proofs:
https://foss.heptapod.net/isa-afp/afp-2021-1

People who have tested previous release candidates should follow RC4. People
who have not participated in testing yet, should try RC4 now. This is probably
the last call.

Any feedback about Isabelle release candidates should be posted with a
meaningful Subject line, not just a clone of this announcement.

Makarius
