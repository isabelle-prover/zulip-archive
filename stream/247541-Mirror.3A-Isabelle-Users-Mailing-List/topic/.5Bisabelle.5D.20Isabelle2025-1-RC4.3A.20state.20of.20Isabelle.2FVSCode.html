No issues from my side regarding the current state. Usability and
stability improved a lot since the Isabelle2025 release, thanks!

Here are 2 things that would further improve my experience:

  • Something like the theories panel from jEdit (so that I can see build
    progress & failures)

  • General active markup support. E.g. when I hit the tracing limit

    messages?"), I cannot click it to see more messages.

Best wishes,

Kevin
