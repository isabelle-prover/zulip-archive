Dear friends and colleagues,

We’re happy to announce that the kick-off meeting of the ERC Synergy project Malinca <http://malinca.org/> will take place in Paris at Institut Henri Poincaré <https://www.ihp.fr/en> (IHP) on October the 1st, the 2nd and the 3rd, 2025.

The ERC project Malinca is a six-year research project whose ambition is to bridge the linguistic gap currently existing between the daily practice of mathematics performed in natural language and the formal mathematics implemented today in interactive proof assistants, such as Agda, Isabelle, Lean, Mizar, Rocq and many others.

The three-days kick-off meeting will consist of invited talks and tutorials, together with informal sessions and demos dedicated to the topics of the Malinca project.
