I am happy to announce two new linked contributions, by Manuel Eberl and Katharina Kreuzer:

Perfect Fields
They define perfect fields using one characterization and show the equivalence to another.
Perfect fields including those of characteristic zero, finite fields and algebraically closed fields.
https://www.isa-afp.org/entries/Perfect_Fields.html

and

Elimination of Repeated Factors Algorithm
This is an algorithm to find the square-free part of polynomials over perfect fields.
https://www.isa-afp.org/entries/Elimination_Of_Repeated_Factors.html
