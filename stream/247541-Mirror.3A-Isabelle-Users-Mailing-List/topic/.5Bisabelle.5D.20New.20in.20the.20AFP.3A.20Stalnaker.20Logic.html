Stalnaker's Epistemic Logic
by Laura P. Gamboa Guzman

This work is a formalization of Stalnaker's epistemic logic with countably many
agents and its soundness and completeness theorems, as well as the equivalence
between the axiomatization of S4 available in the Epistemic Logic theory and the
topological one. It builds on the Epistemic Logic theory.

https://www.isa-afp.org/entries/Stalnaker_Logic.html
