Strict Omega Categories
Anthony Bordg and Adrián Doña Mateo

This theory formalises a definition of strict omega-categories and the strict
omega-category of pasting diagrams. It is the first step towards a formalisation of weak
infinity categories à la Batanin–Leinster.

https://www.isa-afp.org/entries/StrictOmegaCategories.html
