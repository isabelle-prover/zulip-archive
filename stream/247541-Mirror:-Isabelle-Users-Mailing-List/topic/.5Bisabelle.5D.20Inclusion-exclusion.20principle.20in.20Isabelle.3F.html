Yes, it's called card_UNION and it's part of the standard library.

lemma card_UNION:
  assumes "finite A"
    and "∀k ∈ A. finite k"
  shows "card (⋃A) = nat (∑I | I ⊆ A ∧ I ≠ {}. (- 1) ^ (card I + 1) *
int (card (⋂I)))"
