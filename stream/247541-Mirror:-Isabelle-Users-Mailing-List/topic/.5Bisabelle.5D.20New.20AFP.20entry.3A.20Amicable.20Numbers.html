Amicable Numbers
by Angeliki Koutsoukou-Argyraki

This is a formalisation of Amicable Numbers, involving some relevant
material including Euler's sigma function, some relevant definitions,
results and examples as well as rules such as Thābit ibn Qurra's Rule,
Euler's Rule, te Riele's Rule and Borho's Rule with breeders.

https://www.isa-afp.org/entries/Amicable_Numbers.html
