Dear all,

In November, I sent an email to this mailing list requesting responses to a survey about the Archive of Formal Proofs. I am happy to announce that the results have been formally written up and are available at https://arxiv.org/abs/2104.01052

In short, the participants were generally satisfied with the AFP, however there are specific areas which can be improved. I would like to thank the 30 participants for their feedback, and I hope the results are of interest to the community.
