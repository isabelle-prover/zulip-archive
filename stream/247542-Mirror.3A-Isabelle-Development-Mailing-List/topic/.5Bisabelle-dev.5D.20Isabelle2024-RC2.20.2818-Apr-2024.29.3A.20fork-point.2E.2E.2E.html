It should be clear that changesets intended for Isabelle2024 should be sent
exclusively to me, and not pushed onto the post-release isabelle-dev repository.

When the isabelle-release repository is eventually merged back onto
isabelle-dev that will cause ugly effects due to self-conflicts of duplicate
copies. It will ultimately work out, but is like pouring used motor oil onto a
white shirt.

Isabelle history is meant to be readable and understandable later, unlike the
average project on Github.
