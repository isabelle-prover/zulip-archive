Bryan Ford (Aug 05 2025 at 13:48):

In case it may be still of interest, as part of a project to formalize and metalogically analyze a logic we're calling grounded deduction and an associated system of "grounded arithmetic", we've also formalized most of Skolem's primitive recursive arithmetic (PRA) and significant parts of Peano arithmetic (PA) in a common formal language framework atop Isabelle/HOL. It's not released yet, but we're considering making it available publicly sometime soon, and in the meantime are happy to share access privately if there's interest.
