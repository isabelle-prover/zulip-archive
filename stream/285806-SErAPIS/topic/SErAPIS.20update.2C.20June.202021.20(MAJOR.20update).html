Topic: SErAPIS update, June 2021 (MAJOR update)

Yiannos (Jun 23 2021 at 10:18):

Dear all,

We would like to thank all of you who have so far given us feedback on your search results using the SErAPIS search engine for the Isabelle Libraries and AFP (https://behemoth.cl.cam.ac.uk/search/).

Today, we are excited to announce a major update to the SErAPIS Isabelle Search engine.
Changes and updates include:

  • Index updated to include the AFP up to and including the 31/05/2021 snapshot.
  • Improved indexing of mathematical concepts.
  • Added two new search algorithms, methods 7 and 8 (method 8 is now default) that we invite you to try out.
  • Improved search with fact names as keywords.
  • Optimisations to the index and overall performance of search.

and more.

We would like to remind you that there two short SErAPIS introductory videos on our YouTube channel:

https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCrpChEhaz7FS_6Q7gtzFlFQ

and a detailed user guide available here:

https://behemoth.cl.cam.ac.uk/search/SErAPIS_online_user_guide.pdf

Finally, we kindly ask that you continue to give us feedback on (at least) the first 10 results of your searches via the SErAPIS interface (as explained in Video 2 and Section 4 of the user guide).

All data collected is anonymised.

We are very grateful to all of you for using SErAPIS and for your feedback.

Thank you!

Yiannos Stathopoulos and Angeliki Koutsoukou-Argyraki
