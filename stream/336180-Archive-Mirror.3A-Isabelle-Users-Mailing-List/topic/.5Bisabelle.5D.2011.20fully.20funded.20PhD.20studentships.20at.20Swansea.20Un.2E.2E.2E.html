There are 11 fully funded PhD studentships (4 years, including an integrated masters) available in the EPSRC CENTRE FOR DOCTORAL TRAINING IN ENHANCING COLLABORATIONS AND INTERACTIONS WITH DATA AND INTELLIGENCE DRIVEN SYSTEMS (http://people-first.best <http://people-first.best/>) at Swansea University. The centre takes an interdisciplinary approach and involves besides computer science and mathematics also engineering, life science, law, management. The computational expertise and training in the Centre spans and integrates a broad range of theoretical to experimental science.

Application deadline: March 22nd, 2019.
