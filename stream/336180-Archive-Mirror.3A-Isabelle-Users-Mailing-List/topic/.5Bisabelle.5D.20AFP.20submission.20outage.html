Dear potential AFP submitters,

due to a server outage, the AFP submission system will be unavailable
for a while, presumable until this Friday (29 May).

If you have an urgent submission that cannot wait until the system is
back up again, feel free to submit it as a .tar.gz file via email to
afp-submit@in.tum.de, including meta information:

  • list of authors (possibly with email addresses and web sites if you
    wish for those to appear on the web site)

  • abstract (usually the same as the LaTeX abstract in root.tex)

  • list of topics (as listed in the AFP Index)
  • whether you want BSD or LGPL licence
  • email address of the person(s) who will maintain the entry

Cheers,

Manuel
