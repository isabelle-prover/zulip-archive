The new AFP entry that I just anounced

contributors for making the AFP such an amazing collection of formalizations

(No Faster-Than-Light Observers). In total, the AFP now contains 2.300.000 lines
of Isabelle, proving almost 140.000 lemmas, contributed over 15 years.
