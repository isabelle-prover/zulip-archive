Uppsala University, Sweden, advertises a tenure-track position as
assistant professor in Computing Science. A candidate should have a PhD
in computer science with research merits in one of the following areas:
design and implementation of programming languages, especially with
respect to parallelism and concurrency, handling of large data volumes,
including information extraction, combinatorial optimisation, formal
methods and computer science didactics. Teaching duties will include
Software Engineering. Closing date for applications is August 15, 2019.
