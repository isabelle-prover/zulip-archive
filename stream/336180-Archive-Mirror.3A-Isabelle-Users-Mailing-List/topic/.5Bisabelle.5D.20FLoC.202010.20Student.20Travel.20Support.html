FLoC has received funds to provide travel grants of up to $750 for student attendees of FLoC'10. Funds can be requested to cover airfare and lodging. We expect to award about 100 grants.

The application deadline is May 3, 2010, and recipients will be notified by May 15. Awardees are required to register to FLoC by the early registration deadline (May 17) and stay at the conference housing (Pollock Halls).

More info at http://www.floc-conference.org/floc-student-grants.html
