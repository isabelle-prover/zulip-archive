FINAL CALL FOR PARTICIPATION
ITP 2019
The Tenth International Conference on Interactive Theorem Proving
8 to 13 September 2019 in Portland, Oregon, USA

https://itp19.cecs.pdx.edu/

Main conference: 9 September to 12 September
Affiliated events: 8 September and 13 September

ITP is the premier international conference for researchers from all
areas of interactive theorem proving and its applications.

June Andronick (Data61|CSIRO and The University of New South Wales)
Martin Dixon (Intel)

https://itp19.cecs.pdx.edu/accepted-papers/

The following affiliated events will take place on either side of the
main conference:

  • SpISA 2019: Workshop on Instruction Set Architecture Specification
    (Friday, 13 September)

  • ProofGround: Interactive Proving Contests (Friday, 13 September)
