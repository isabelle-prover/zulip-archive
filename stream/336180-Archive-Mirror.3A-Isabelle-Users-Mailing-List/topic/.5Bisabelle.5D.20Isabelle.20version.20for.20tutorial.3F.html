Hi Aaron,

Numerous changes have happened since then, mostly improvements ;-)
Nothing really major (nat is still nat and list still list), but the
many small changes (eg the theory header) are likely to annoy novices
considerably. Thus it is probably better to use the old version. I
suspect that for novices the lack of the new features is less crucial
than for power users.
