Bug in Emacs 23.3 (I'm not sure exactly when it was introduced), fixed in 23.4.

http://proofgeneral.inf.ed.ac.uk/trac/ticket/318
http://debbugs.gnu.org/cgi/bugreport.cgi?bug=8915

Workaround: keep some whitespace at the end of your buffer, after the symbol you are trying to type.
