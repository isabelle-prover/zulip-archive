In the "system" manual there is section 2.3 "Invoking the build process",
which starts with a copy of the command-line usage, with some extra
explanations. (This is a general principle for most Isabelle command-line
tools.)

Moreover, at the end of the section there are various examples.

The subsequent chapter in the manual is then about the whole complex of
document preparation.
