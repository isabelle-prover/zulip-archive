Email Gateway (Aug 19 2022 at 09:59):

From: Ramana Kumar <rk436@cam.ac.uk>
Do you need it to be computable?
Otherwise just use choice: SOME x. x ∈ S ∧ ∀y. y ∈ S ⇒ y ≤ x
This may already be defined in some library.
