I am not aware of Isabelle 2010, most likely you mean the Isabelle
version which was released in 2010, which was Isabelle2009-2. Several
recent Isabelle releases are available from

http://isabelle.in.tum.de/download_past.html

which is admittedly not that easy to find:

1) http://isabelle.in.tum.de
2) -> Installation
3) -> archive

On the website of the desired release (assuming it is Isabelle2009-2)
there is "Download & Support" with a link to "installation instructions".
