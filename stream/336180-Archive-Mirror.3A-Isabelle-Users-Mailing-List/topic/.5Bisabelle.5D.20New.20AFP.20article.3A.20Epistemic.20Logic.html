Epistemic Logic
Andreas Halkjær From

This work is a formalization of epistemic logic with countably many agents. It
includes proofs of soundness and completeness for the axiom system K. The
completeness proof is based on the textbook "Reasoning About Knowledge" by
Fagin, Halpern, Moses and Vardi (MIT Press 1995).

https://www.isa-afp.org/entries/Epistemic_Logic.html
