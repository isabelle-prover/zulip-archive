Euler's Partition Theorem
Lukas Bulwahn

Euler's Partition Theorem states that the number of partitions with only
distinct parts is equal to the number of partitions with only odd parts. The
combinatorial proof follows John Harrison's HOL Light formalization. This
theorem is the 45th theorem of the Top 100 Theorems list.

http://afp.sourceforge.net/entries/Euler_Partition.shtml
