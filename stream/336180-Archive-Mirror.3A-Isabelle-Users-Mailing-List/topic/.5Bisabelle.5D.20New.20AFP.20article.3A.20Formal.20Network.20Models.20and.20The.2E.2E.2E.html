Formal Network Models and Their Application to Firewall Policies
Achim D. Brucker, Lukas Brügger and Burkhart Wolff

We present a formal model of network protocols and their application to modeling
firewall policies. The formalization is based on the Unified Policy Framework
(UPF). The formalization was originally developed with for generating test cases
for testing the security configuration actual firewall and router (middle-boxes)
using HOL-TestGen. Our work focuses on modeling application level protocols on
top of tcp/ip.

https://www.isa-afp.org/entries/UPF_Firewall.shtml
