Positional Determinacy of Parity Games
Christoph Dittmann

We present a formalization of parity games (a two-player game on directed
graphs) and a proof of their positional determinacy in Isabelle/HOL. This proof
works for both finite and infinite games.

http://afp.sourceforge.net/entries/Parity_Game.shtml

This is a fundamental article that gave rise to the new category Computer
Science / Games. We look forward to more articles from this rich field!

Thank you Christoph.
