No Faster-Than-Light Observers
Mike Stannett and István Németi

We provide a formal proof within First Order Relativity Theory that no observer
can travel faster than the speed of light. Originally reported in Stannett &
Németi (2014) "Using Isabelle/HOL to verify first-order relativity theory",
Journal of Automated Reasoning 52(4), pp. 361-378.

http://www.isa-afp.org/entries/No_FTL_observers.shtml
