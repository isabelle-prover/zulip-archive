PHD STUDENTSHIP
Formal Specification and Certification in Automated Deduction

Closing date: May 15, 2008
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

The Research Group CASSIS/INRIA Nancy Grand Est (France) invites
applications for a three-year-funded PhD studentship in Formal
Specification and Certification in Automated Deduction. We are looking
for candidates with a good honours degree in Theoretical Computer
Science, Formal Verification/Validation or Mathematics, with
programming skills and some experience in Automated Deduction.

Required skills:

  • Master's degree in Computer Science
  • experience in Automated Deduction
  • good command of the English language

Desirable skills:

  • experience in application of formal methods to system validation
  • familiarity with Coq and/or CAML

Further details can be found at:

http://www.inria.fr/travailler/opportunites/doc.en.html
