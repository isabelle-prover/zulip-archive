SSV 2011: Call for Participation

6th International Workshop on
Systems Software Verification (SSV'11)
Real Software, Real Problems, Real Solutions

August 26, 2011, Nijmegen, The Netherlands
https://es.fbk.eu/events/ssv2011/

............................................................................

SSV'11 is the 6th workshop in a series bringing together researchers and
developers from both academia and industry, who are facing real software
and real problems to find real, applicable solutions. This years workshop
is co-located with the 2nd International Conference on Interactive Theorem
Proving (ITP'11).

* Invited Talks *

  • Sebastien Bardin (CEA, France)
  • Bart Jacobs (Katholieke Universiteit Leuven, Belgium)
  • Peter Sewell (University of Cambridge, UK)

* Registration Deadlines *

  • Early registration (before July 15 2011), EUR 40
  • Late registration (after July 15 2011), EUR 50
    Please follow directions and links on the website.

* Location *

Hotel Erica in Berg en Dal (next to Nijmegen, the Netherlands)
