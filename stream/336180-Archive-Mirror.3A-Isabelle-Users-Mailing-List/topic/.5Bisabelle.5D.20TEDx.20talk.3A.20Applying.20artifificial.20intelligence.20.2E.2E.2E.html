At the end of May, Colin Rowat gave a TEDx
<http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/TED_(conference)#TEDx> talk on mechanised
reasoning and our applications of it to auctions.

The video is now available at https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O65XJd7j2BE.
The underlying papers referred to in the talk are:

  1. implementations of Vickrey’s theorem in four different provers:
    http://arxiv.org/abs/1303.4193

  2. proving soundness of auction designs in Isabelle:
    http://arxiv.org/abs/1308.1779
