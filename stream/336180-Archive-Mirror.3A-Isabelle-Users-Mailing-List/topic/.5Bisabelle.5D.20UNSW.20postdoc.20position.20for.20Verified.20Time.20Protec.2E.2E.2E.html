The Trustworthy Systems group in Australia has an open post doc position at UNSW, Sydney.

The position is to support the Verified Time Protection project, which is funded by an ARC Discovery grant. It aims to provably prevent information leakage through timing channels, by providing principled temporal isolation in the operating system (OS).

We’re planning to make Spectre and friends a thing of the past.

More information and online application at:
http://external-careers.jobs.unsw.edu.au/cw/en/job/497264/research-associate-computer-science-formal-methods

Applications close 18 Aug 2019.
