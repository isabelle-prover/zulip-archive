Hi Gergely,

You can use (<) and (>) to hide parts of your theories.

Like:

(<)

... hidden stuff ...

(>)

Another options are command tags, see Section 4.3 in the Isabelle
reference manual.

lemma X: ...
proof %invisible (induct x)
...
qed
