Dear Yucy,

apply (relation "measure (\<lambda>(i,j). j + 1 - i)")

Your function has three arguments, so your measure function must be on
triples. Then it works:

termination
by (relation "measure (\<lambda>(i,j,_). j + 1 - i)") auto

Alex
