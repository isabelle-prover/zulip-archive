From: Alfio Martini <alfio.martini@acm.org>
Hi Giampaolo,

It is very simple if you use antiquotations, like for instance:

@{subgoals [display,indent =10]}

You may want to take at look at the reference manual, page 41 (and the
tutorial, page 61)

Best!
